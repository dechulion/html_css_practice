About Me

It's always hard to know what to write about oneself. Each individual has so many different apsects that trying to smash them all into a few little paragraphs or even an entire webpage is not doing justice to the diversity found even at the individual level. I thrive on excitement, hence the interest in skydiving. However, I also find enjoyment in sitting queitly and observing the world around me. I like to be social, to get silly with friends and make a racous (check out the pirate battle pics). Yet a just as much enjoy being a homebody, reading a good book or playing on the computer.

I guess for me the key is balance. The ability to truly experience life by involving yourself in all of it's diversity. To focus just on one area is to loose perspective. You box yourself in and narrow your own definition. And that's just no fun.
