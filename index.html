Defending Wild Washington

Defending Wild Washington was a collaborative effort written between 17 students and 2 professors of The Evergreen State College. The book details both the environmental history of Washington State, and the history of the citizens efforts to protect the lands natural beauty. It then goes on to discuss what can still be done to help keep the Evergreen State green. My efforts included co-authoring Chapter 2, detailing the environmental changes Euro-American's made to Washington's landscape and Chapter 4, accounting the current socioeconomic conditions of the state.
